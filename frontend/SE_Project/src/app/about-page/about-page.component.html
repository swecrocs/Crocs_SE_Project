About The Grid

A research collaboration platform designed to connect graduate students, postdocs, and faculty across universities.

Our Goal

The Grid aims to break down institutional boundaries and make it easier for researchers to collaborate, share ideas, and create impactful work together. We believe in interdisciplinary discovery through open academic networks.

Features

Project Collaboration

Create, manage, and join collaborative research projects with ease.

Search & Discovery

Find collaborators and projects based on expertise and interests.

Cross-Institution Networking

Build connections beyond your university and discipline.
